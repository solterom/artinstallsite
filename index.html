About

Taking the stress out of hanging

Whether it's a single treasured piece or an entire gallery wall, we make sure your pictures are hung securely, level, and exactly where they look best.

Based in Christchurch, we bring the right tools, hardware, and experience to every job so you don't have to worry about finding studs, patching mistakes, or ending up with wonky pictures.

From family photos to large-scale artworks, mirrors to floating shelves; if it goes on a wall, we'll get it right.
